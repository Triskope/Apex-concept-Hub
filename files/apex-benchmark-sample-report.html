Section 3 · Plan design improvements

Changes worth considering

Opportunity

Cross-tested or cash-balance combo design

At a similar employer cost, owner and key-employee savings could roughly double — from $31,000 toward the $65,000–$70,000+ range peers achieve. A design study models this precisely before any change.

Opportunity

Adopt automatic enrollment

Lifts participation, supports nondiscrimination testing, and is increasingly standard in peer plans — low effort, durable benefit.

Opportunity

Revisit the match formula

A tiered or stretch-match formula can deliver the same employer dollars with better participation behavior and testing outcomes.

Section 5 · Summary & recommendations

Recommendations

Rebid recordkeeping and review fund share classes. The plan's largest savings opportunity — peer pricing suggests roughly $10–13K/yr is available without changing what participants experience.

Model a cross-tested or cash-balance combo design. At a similar employer cost, owner and key-employee savings could roughly double. Worth a design study before the next plan year.

Adopt the two housekeeping fixes. Confirm the document amendments and align the compensation definition — small items now, audit friction later.
